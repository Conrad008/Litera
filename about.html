Welcome to litera


Who are We?


We are a newly established bookstore based in nairobi mombasa and eldoret cities with our main branch in westlands nairobi.

our mission

Our mission is simple : books for all.
We believe that in order to raise a nation of capable leaders and vissionaries, we must first incculcated in our children and everybody with the right teachings and philosophies.
in part with this we are striving to make the neccesary resource i.e: books available to each and every person in our country.
By making our books not only available in person but also online parents, institutions and other person interested in building an innate reading culture within them will find it easier to get started due to the availability of materials

our vision

To have everyone everywhere have access to books
